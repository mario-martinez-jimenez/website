Hi — I’m Mario Martínez‑Jiménez.

Imperial College Research Fellow (Dept. of Economics & Public Policy, Imperial Business School) and Visiting Postdoctoral Scholar (Department of Health Policy, Stanford School of Medicine, 2025–2026).

My research sits at the intersection of health, labor, and public economics, with an emphasis on child development, human capital formation, healthcare inequality, and family outcomes. Current projects study (i) long‑term effects of early‑life shocks, (ii) environmental risks shaping young people’s mental health and well‑being, (iii) socioeconomic inequality in health and access to care, and (iv) factors behind declining fertility.

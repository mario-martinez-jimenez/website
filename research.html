Research

Work in Progress

  • Early‑life conditions and children’s adaptive behaviour in the UKHLS (with E. Aguirre) — Working paper, draft. [link]
    [abstract]

    Examines how perinatal and early‑childhood conditions shape communication, physical, and socio‑emotional adaptive behaviour by age three, using UKHLS with causal‑inference strategies.

  • Temporary contracts and fertility in Spain’s 1984 reform (with J. Vall Castello) — Working paper, draft. [link]
    [abstract]

    Evaluates how exposure to temporary employment at labour‑market entry affects completed fertility and timing of births, combining birth registries with survey data.

  • NICU admission and later educational outcomes in Ireland (with O. Doyle) — Working paper, draft. [link]
    [abstract]

    Longitudinal evidence on educational challenges among children admitted to NICU, leveraging Growing Up in Ireland cohorts.

  • Medicaid vaccination mandates and paediatric uptake in the US (with M. Rossin‑Slater, P. Persson) — Working paper, draft. [link]
    [abstract]

    Uses MAX/TAF claims and policy variation to estimate causal effects of school‑entry mandates and exemptions on vaccine uptake.

  • Economic shocks, retirement, and mental health (with B. Hollingsworth, E. Zucchelli) — Working paper, draft. [link]
    [abstract]

    IV panel analysis on how retirement interacts with macro shocks to influence mental health (ELSA data).

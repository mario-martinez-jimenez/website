Selected work

Projects in progress and published articles spanning labour markets, child development, and health inequality.

Work in progress

  • Early-life conditions and children’s adaptive behaviour in the UKHLS (with E. Aguirre) — Working paper, draft stage.draft
    [abstract]

    Examines how perinatal and early-childhood conditions shape communication, physical, and socio-emotional adaptive behaviour by age three, using UKHLS data and causal-inference strategies.

  • Temporary contracts and fertility in Spain’s 1984 reform (with J. Vall Castello) — Working paper, draft stage.draft
    [abstract]

    Evaluates how exposure to temporary employment at labour-market entry affects completed fertility and timing of births, combining birth registries with survey data.

  • NICU admission and later educational outcomes in Ireland (with O. Doyle) — Working paper, draft stage.draft
    [abstract]

    Provides longitudinal evidence on educational challenges among children admitted to neonatal intensive care units, leveraging Growing Up in Ireland cohorts.

  • Medicaid vaccination mandates and paediatric uptake in the US (with M. Rossin-Slater, P. Persson) — Working paper, draft stage.draft
    [abstract]

    Uses MAX/TAF claims and policy variation to estimate causal effects of school-entry mandates and exemptions on vaccine uptake.

  • Economic shocks, retirement, and mental health (with B. Hollingsworth, E. Zucchelli) — Working paper, draft stage.draft
    [abstract]

    Applies instrumental-variable panel methods to study how retirement interacts with macroeconomic shocks to influence mental health using ELSA data.
